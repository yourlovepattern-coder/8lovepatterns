Privacy Policy

What we collect, why, for how long, and the rights you have over your data.

Who we are

8LovePatterns is operated by ROWV AND CO, a French micro-entrepreneur business. Contact: [support@8lovepatterns.com].

What we collect

  • Your email address, if you provide one to receive your report or communications.
  • Your answers to the test questionnaire, used only to generate your personal report.
  • Payment information, handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Basic, privacy-respecting analytics (PostHog, hosted in the EU) about how the site is used, to help us improve it.

Why we collect it

Solely to deliver the product you requested (your report), to send you order-related communications, and to understand and improve the site. We do not sell your data, and we do not use your test answers for advertising.

Order and payment records are kept for the period required by French accounting law (currently 10 years for commercial records). Test answers not linked to a completed purchase are not retained beyond what's needed to generate your immediate result.

If you are in the European Union, the UK, or California, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict how we use it. To exercise any of these rights, email [support@8lovepatterns.com]. EU residents may also lodge a complaint with the CNIL (France's data protection authority) if unsatisfied with our response.

Stripe (payment processing), Resend (transactional email delivery), Cloudflare (hosting and analytics proxy), PostHog (privacy-focused analytics, EU-hosted).
